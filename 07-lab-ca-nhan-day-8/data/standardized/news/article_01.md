 Chia sẻ

 Ca sĩ Chi Dân, người mẫu An Tây và TikToker Nguyễn Đỗ Trúc Phương bị Công an TP.HCM khởi tố, bắt giam để điều tra về hành vi 'tổ chức sử dụng trái phép chất ma túy'. 

 Ng&agrave;y 14.10, C&ocirc;ng an TP.HCM cung c&#7845;p th&ocirc;ng tin v&#7909; c&aacute;c b&#7883; can mua ma t&uacute;y &#273;&#7875; t&#7893; ch&#7913;c s&#7917; d&#7909;ng tr&aacute;i ph&eacute;p ch&#7845;t ma t&uacute;y . 
 Ba&#774;&#769;t giam ca si&#771; Chi Da&#770;n, ngu&#795;o&#795;&#768;i ma&#770;&#771;u An Ta&#770;y, TikToker Tru&#769;c Phu&#795;o&#795;ng do lie&#770;n quan ma tu&#769;y

 C&ocirc;ng an TP.HCM &#273;&atilde; kh&#7903;i t&#7889; b&#7883; can, b&#7855;t t&#7841;m giam ng&#432;&#7901;i m&#7851;u An T&acirc;y, ca s&#297; Chi D&acirc;n v&agrave; Nguy&#7877;n &#272;&#7895; Tr&uacute;c Ph&#432;&#417;ng v&#7873; h&agrave;nh vi t&#7893; ch&#7913;c s&#7917; d&#7909;ng tr&aacute;i ph&eacute;p ch&#7845;t ma t&uacute;y; ri&ecirc;ng ng&#432;&#7901;i m&#7851;u An T&acirc;y c&ograve;n b&#7883; kh&#7903;i t&#7889; th&ecirc;m v&#7873; h&agrave;nh vi t&agrave;ng tr&#7919; tr&aacute;i ph&eacute;p ch&#7845;t ma t&uacute;y. 

 C&ocirc;ng an TP.HCM kh&#7903;i t&#7889;, b&#7855;t t&#7841;m giam Nguy&#7877;n Th&#7883; An (t&#7913;c ng&#432;&#7901;i m&#7851;u An T&acirc;y)
 C&ocirc;ng an TP.HCM cho bi&#7871;t, qu&aacute;n tri&#7879;t th&#7921;c hi&#7879;n ch&#7881; &#273;&#7841;o c&#7911;a l&atilde;nh &#273;&#7841;o B&#7897; C&ocirc;ng an v&agrave; Th&#7911; t&#432;&#7899;ng Ch&iacute;nh ph&#7911; v&#7873; ti&#7871;p t&#7909;c ph&aacute;t huy nh&#7919;ng th&agrave;nh t&iacute;ch &#273;&atilde; &#273;&#7841;t &#273;&#432;&#7907;c trong &#273;i&#7873;u tra, m&#7903; r&#7897;ng, truy x&eacute;t to&agrave;n b&#7897; &#273;&#432;&#7901;ng d&acirc;y t&#7897;i ph&#7841;m v&#7853;n chuy&#7875;n tr&aacute;i ph&eacute;p ch&#7845;t ma t&uacute;y qua &#273;&#432;&#7901;ng h&agrave;ng kh&ocirc;ng t&#7915; Ph&aacute;p v&#7873; Vi&#7879;t Nam v&agrave;o ng&agrave;y 16.3.2023, C&ocirc;ng an TP.HCM ti&#7871;p t&#7909;c &#273;&#7849;y m&#7841;nh &#273;&#7845;u tranh, m&#7903; r&#7897;ng chuy&ecirc;n &aacute;n; l&#7847;n theo d&ograve;ng ch&#7843;y ma t&uacute;y &#273;&#7875; khui t&#7915;ng v&#7887; b&#7885;c c&#7911;a c&aacute;c &ocirc;ng tr&ugrave;m ma t&uacute;y.
 Qua &#273;&oacute;, tri&#7879;t ph&aacute; th&ecirc;m 146 &#273;&#432;&#7901;ng d&acirc;y, b&#259;ng nh&oacute;m mua b&aacute;n, v&#7853;n chuy&#7875;n tr&aacute;i ph&eacute;p ch&#7845;t ma t&uacute;y ho&#7841;t &#273;&#7897;ng li&ecirc;n qu&#7853;n, huy&#7879;n, li&ecirc;n t&#7881;nh, th&agrave;nh ph&#7889;, kh&#7903;i t&#7889; th&ecirc;m 630 b&#7883; can.

 B&#7883; can Nguy&#7877;n &#272;&#7895; Tr&uacute;c Ph&#432;&#417;ng
 K&#7871;t qu&#7843; &#273;&#7845;u tranh chuy&ecirc;n &aacute;n &#273;&#7871;n nay &#273;&atilde; tri&#7879;t ph&aacute; g&#7847;n 500 &#273;&#432;&#7901;ng d&acirc;y, b&#259;ng nh&oacute;m t&#7897;i ph&#7841;m v&#7873; ma t&uacute;y, kh&#7903;i t&#7889; 1.132 b&#7883; can; x&#7917; ph&#7841;t h&agrave;nh ch&iacute;nh v&agrave; l&#7853;p h&#7891; s&#417; &#273;&#432;a &#273;i cai nghi&#7879;n 201 ng&#432;&#7901;i, thu gi&#7919; 323,5 kg ma t&uacute;y c&aacute;c lo&#7841;i, 12 kh&#7849;u s&uacute;ng, 3 qu&#7843; l&#7921;u &#273;&#7841;n v&agrave; nhi&#7873;u v&#7853;t ch&#7913;ng c&oacute; li&ecirc;n quan.
 B&#432;&#7899;c &#273;&#7847;u, c&#417; quan &#273;i&#7873;u tra &#273;&atilde; l&agrave;m r&otilde;, c&aacute;c nghi ph&#7841;m, nghi can &#273;&atilde; giao d&#7883;ch mua b&aacute;n ma t&uacute;y v&#7899;i s&#7889; ti&#7873;n h&#417;n 28.000 t&#7881; &#273;&#7891;ng.

 &#272;&aacute;ng ch&uacute; &yacute;, C&ocirc;ng an TP.HCM &#273;&atilde; truy x&eacute;t, x&#7917; l&yacute; tri&#7879;t &#273;&#7875; c&aacute;c nghi ph&#7841;m mua ma t&uacute;y &#273;&#7875; t&#7893; ch&#7913;c s&#7917; d&#7909;ng tr&aacute;i ph&eacute;p ch&#7845;t ma t&uacute;y, trong &#273;&oacute; c&oacute; ng&#432;&#7901;i m&#7851;u, di&#7877;n vi&ecirc;n, ca s&#297; v&agrave; nh&#7919;ng ng&#432;&#7901;i c&oacute; &#7843;nh h&#432;&#7903;ng l&#7899;n tr&ecirc;n m&#7841;ng x&atilde; h&#7897;i , nh&#432;: ca s&#297; Chi D&acirc;n , ng&#432;&#7901;i m&#7851;u An T&acirc;y , Nguy&#7877;n &#272;&#7895; Tr&uacute;c Ph&#432;&#417;ng... Qua &#273;&oacute; &#273;&atilde; kh&#7903;i t&#7889; b&#7883; can, ra l&#7879;nh b&#7855;t t&#7841;m giam c&aacute;c b&#7883; can n&agrave;y &#273;&#7875; ti&#7871;p t&#7909;c &#273;i&#7873;u tra m&#7903; r&#7897;ng v&#7909; &aacute;n.

N&#259;m 2023, m&#7841;ng x&atilde; h&#7897;i c&#361;ng lan truy&#7873;n h&igrave;nh &#7843;nh ca s&#297; Chi D&acirc;n &#273;&#432;&#7907;c cho l&agrave; b&#7883; c&#417; quan ch&#7913;c n&#259;ng m&#7901;i l&ecirc;n l&agrave;m r&otilde; v&igrave; nghi li&ecirc;n quan &#273;&#7871;n s&#7917; d&#7909;ng ma t&uacute;y. Tuy nhi&ecirc;n, th&#7901;i &#273;i&#7875;m n&agrave;y, ph&iacute;a &#273;&#7841;i di&#7879;n nam ca s&#297; &#273;&atilde; b&aacute;c b&#7887; th&ocirc;ng tin n&agrave;y.
